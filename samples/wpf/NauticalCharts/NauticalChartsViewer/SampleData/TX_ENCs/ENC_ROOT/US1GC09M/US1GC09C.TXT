Navigation regulations are published in Chapter 2, U.S. Coast Pilot 4 and 5. Additions or revisions to Chapter 2 are published in the Local Notice to Mariners. Information concerning the regulations may be obtained at the Office of the Commander, 7th Coast Guard District in Miami, FL and 8th Coast Guard District in New Orleans, LA, or at the Office of the District Engineer, Corps of Engineers, in Charleston, SC, Jacksonville, FL, Mobile, AL, New Orleans, LA and Galveston, TX.
